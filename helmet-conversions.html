Cairns Style Brass Band Conversion

The comfort of your traditional leather helmet without the weight. A conversion changes how your helmet's brass hardware is built rather than just repairing what's there. Choose a package — Basic Brass Band Conversion, Custom Conversion, or Premium New Build — or pick "Not sure — need advice" and I'll help you land on the right one. Submit a conversion order below with a photo of your current helmet and, if you have one, a reference photo of the style you're after.

One note up front: if your helmet is OSHA-compliant, it can't be converted to NFPA compliance after the fact — that rating is fixed at original certification. I'll flag this during review if it applies to your order, but it helps to know going in.
